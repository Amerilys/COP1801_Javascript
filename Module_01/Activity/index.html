Marianne Dankner

Marianne is a Sophmore at Rasmussen College on her way to graduating in June 2018. She works as a Ghost Writer and a Graphics Designer. Marianne enjoys helping Twitch Streamers create modern content by providing them with custom overlays and webdesigns. She spends her free time with her daughter Elaina and enjoys going on hikes together.


Marianne found a love for programming back in the early 00's when she fell in love with the PC game Halo: Combat Evolved. She became a part of the modding community and helped produce several mods for the game. By the mid 2000's she had migrated to web design as a way to build websites for those who needed places to host their content. She soon fell out of practice while overcoming illness but has returned to school to further her education and pursue her love of programming.
